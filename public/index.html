WhatsApp Downloader Bot

Connect your WhatsApp number to use download features

How to connect:

  1. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  2. Go to Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device
  3. Enter the pairing code above

Available Commands

YouTube Video Download

Send !yt [YouTube URL] to get the video

YouTube Audio Download

Send !ytmp3 [YouTube URL] to get the audio

TikTok Video Download

Send !tiktok [TikTok URL] to get the video
